What is Forest Sentinel?

Forest Sentinel is a Reforestation Monitoring and Management System (RMMS) that blends technology and community. We help forest managers and environmental NGOs track tree growth, soil health, and biodiversity in real-time.

IoT Integration

Collect real-time data on temperature, humidity, and soil conditions directly from in-field sensors.

Drone & GIS Mapping

Use high-resolution drone imagery and GIS mapping to visualize forest growth and coverage.

Community Engagement

Empower local communities to contribute field reports and observations via a public mobile app.

Our Purpose

We're bridging the gap between cutting-edge technology and on-the-ground environmental action. Our goal is to create a comprehensive and collaborative platform for all stakeholders.

Real-Time Insights

Get instant alerts and up-to-the-minute data on environmental conditions to make faster, more informed decisions.

Community Collaboration

Foster a sense of shared responsibility by empowering local citizens to actively participate in conservation efforts.

Data-Driven Decisions

Move beyond guesswork with comprehensive reports and analytics that guide strategic planning for a healthier planet.

Key Features
